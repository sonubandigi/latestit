What is Business Analysis?

Business Analysis is the process of understanding the business needs. A Business Analyst acts as a liaison between the technical group (software development group) and the Client (Business). A BA elicits the requirements, analyzes, documents them, models the requirements and communicates those requirements to the Technical team.

Business Analyst training program

If you're planning to move your career into IT Sector and if you have good communication skills, Managerial abilities and working knowledge of any Business Process in any sector, you have definitely come to the right place!

We are giving training on Business Analyst role and responsibilities. Business Analyst training is best suited for Resources who want to shift their career to IT from any vertical. Along with the theory, we also cover the practical aspects of a Business Analyst. Business Analyst Training with us will be like an On-Job Training as the Candidate will be working live on the same concepts practiced during the training.

Benefits of BA?

Business Analyst jobs are now in huge demand and highly paid jobs. We provide the best training in Business Analysis to corporate and the individuals and have placed many candidates in our Hyderabad location.

The workshop is conducted for 32 hours duration which is conducted during weekends (Consecutive Saturday's and Sunday's) to benefit the working professionals.

Previous work experience in a Domain or Process or IT is considered relevant experience to a Business Analyst. Candidate need not start the career afresh, it will be a continuation from the previous role. Once you equip yourself with BA Skills, you can start attending BA interviews. Good Communication Skills will be an added advantage. Show case the confidence of driving a project will make you get into BA role.
